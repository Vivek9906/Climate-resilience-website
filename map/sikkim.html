Sikkim Pollution Information

Here you can find information about pollution levels in Sikkim.

Cause of Pollution:

Forest Fires: Sikkim faces significant air pollution due to forest fires, especially during the dry season. These fires release smoke, particulate matter, and other pollutants into the air.
Vehicle Emissions: Increasing vehicular traffic, particularly in urban areas like Gangtok, leads to emissions of carbon monoxide (CO), nitrogen oxides (NOx), and particulate matter, contributing to air pollution.
Industrial Activities: Industrial areas in Sikkim emit pollutants such as sulfur dioxide (SO2), nitrogen oxides (NOx), and volatile organic compounds (VOCs), adding to air pollution levels.

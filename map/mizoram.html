Mizoram Pollution Information

Here you can find information about pollution levels in Mizoram.

Cause of Pollution:

Deforestation: Deforestation for agriculture, logging, and infrastructure development is a major contributor to air and water pollution in Mizoram.
Vehicle Emissions: Increasing vehicular traffic, especially in urban areas like Aizawl, leads to emissions of carbon monoxide (CO), nitrogen oxides (NOx), and particulate matter, contributing to air pollution.
Traditional Practices: The burning of biomass for cooking and heating purposes, as well as forest fires, contribute to air pollution by releasing smoke, particulate matter, and other harmful emissions.

The air pollution condition in Mizoram is relatively moderate compared to other states, but efforts are needed to address sources of pollution and maintain good air quality.

Future Outlook

To improve air quality in Mizoram, various measures are being considered:

Promote Sustainable Practices: Encourage sustainable land management practices, reforestation, and afforestation to reduce pollution and preserve natural habitats.
Strengthen Environmental Regulations: Enforce regulations to control deforestation, regulate vehicular emissions, and promote clean energy sources.
Community Engagement: Involve local communities in conservation efforts, raise awareness about the impacts of pollution, and promote eco-friendly practices.
